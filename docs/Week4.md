# Development Log — Week 4

## Objective

Develop a robust method for measuring retinal nerve fiber layer (RNFL) thickness from OCT images.

## Why RNFL?

RNFL thickness is one of the most widely used quantitative biomarkers in retinal imaging and neuro-ophthalmology.

Reliable RNFL measurements require:

- Accurate ONH localization
- Standardized retinal orientation
- Anatomical quadrant generation
